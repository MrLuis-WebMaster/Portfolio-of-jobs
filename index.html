My experience in Perú

I lived one and half years in Lima Perú, so I would like to tell you a little about my experience there. If you want to know Lima, I recommende stroll along the entire beach circuit from Barranco to San Miguel, go to Los Olivos Park in San Isidro, sit in Kennedy Park and enjoy the mystery of the cats, visit the Bridge of the Suspiros, walk through Miraflores. You can't visit Lima and leave before eating the famous Peruvian ceviche and chicha morada.

My experience in Colombia

I'm living in Medellín and I think that this city is very beautiful and nice for many things. The weather is very good, it is never very cold and it is not very hot either. You can enjoy a good coffee in different places of that city like, in Belén Vibras cafe or in Laureles, my favorite place Dèlmuri Cafe. If you are happy like me when you be in the nature, you can visit, Santa Elena, Parque el Salado and Parque Arvi.
